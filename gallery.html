Traditional Indian Art Forms

Traditional Madhubani Art

Beautiful hand-painted Madhubani artwork featuring intricate patterns and vibrant colors from Bihar's rich cultural heritage.

Warli Folk Painting

Ancient tribal art form depicting daily life scenes using simple geometric patterns and earthy tones.

Kalamkari Textile Art

Hand-painted or block-printed cotton textile art from Andhra Pradesh with natural dyes and mythological themes.

Gond Tribal Art

Colorful folk art from Madhya Pradesh featuring dots and lines to create stunning patterns inspired by nature.

Pattachitra Painting

Traditional cloth-based scroll painting from Odisha depicting mythological narratives with intricate details.

Tanjore Glass Painting

Reverse glass painting technique from Tamil Nadu adorned with gold foil and vibrant colors.

Rajasthani Miniature

Delicate miniature painting showcasing royal court scenes with fine brushwork and rich pigments.

Phad Scroll Painting

Large narrative scroll painting from Rajasthan depicting heroic deeds and folklore on cloth.

Kerala Mural Art

Traditional temple mural art with distinctive style, vivid colors, and spiritual themes.

Chittara Art

Indigenous art form from Karnataka featuring geometric patterns and nature-inspired motifs.

Pichwai Painting

Devotional art from Rajasthan depicting Lord Krishna with intricate details and vibrant hues.

Mysore Painting

Classical South Indian painting style characterized by gesso work and gold leaf embellishments.
